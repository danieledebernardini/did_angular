DID Generation and Deletion

From this page you can create or delete a decentralized identifier linked to your account.

To create a DID you must fill in all the fields of the form, then click the "Create DID" button. Upon doing so, the page will update, showing a change in the balance of your account (due to the payed fees) and your decentralized identifier.
If you access this page but you already have a DID then the form will be disabled, and the "Delete DID" button will become enabled

Remember to save the generated DID before you log out, so you can test it in the verification page.
